# Leads - User Guide

## What is a Lead?
A lead is a potential customer who has shown interest in getting a mortgage. They come from various sources like ads, partner referrals, or direct inquiries.

---

## What Can I Do?

### View Leads
- **As a user, I can** see all my leads in a table
- **As a user, I can** switch between "New" tab (only new leads) and "All" tab (all leads)
- **As a user, I can** search leads by name, phone, or email
- **As a user, I can** filter leads by source (e.g., Meta ads, WhatsApp campaigns)
- **As a user, I can** see an SLA timer showing how long until I need to contact a new lead

### View Lead Details
- **As a user, I can** click on any lead to open their details panel
- **As a user, I can** see their contact info (name, email, phone)
- **As a user, I can** see their intent (refinance, purchase, equity release)
- **As a user, I can** see where they came from (source)
- **As a user, I can** see the conversation transcript (if from AskRivo)

### Edit Lead Info
- **As a user, I can** click the pencil icon to edit a lead's details
- **As a user, I can** update their name, email, phone, or intent
- **As a user, I can** save or cancel my changes

### Log Calls
- **As a user, I can** click the phone icon to log a call
- **As a user, I can** select the call outcome:
  - Connected
  - No Answer
  - Busy
  - Wrong Number
  - Switched Off
- **As a user, I can** add notes about what we discussed

### Add Notes
- **As a user, I can** click the note icon to add a note
- **As a user, I can** write any important information about the lead

### Send WhatsApp Messages
- **As a user, I can** click the WhatsApp tab to see message history
- **As a user, I can** send new WhatsApp messages to the lead

### Convert Lead to Client
- **As a user, I can** click "Convert" to turn a qualified lead into a client
- **As a user, I can** add handover notes before converting
- **As a user, I can** click on the client link to view the created client

### Drop Lead
- **As a user, I can** click "Drop" if the lead is not interested
- **As a user, I can** add a reason for dropping

### View Activity History
- **As a user, I can** click the "Activity" tab to see everything that happened
- **As a user, I can** see all calls, notes, and status changes in chronological order

---

## Lead Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **New** | Fresh lead, needs to be contacted |
| **Converted** | Successfully turned into a client |
| **Dropped** | Not interested or not qualified |

---

## Quick Actions (Table Row)

| Icon | Action |
|------|--------|
| Note icon | Add a quick note |
| Phone icon | Log a call |
| "..." menu | Convert or Drop the lead |

---

## Tips
1. Contact new leads quickly - watch the SLA timer!
2. Always log your calls so the team knows what happened
3. Add detailed notes before converting so the next person has context
4. Use the search to quickly find a specific lead
